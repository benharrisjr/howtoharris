Toddler Toys

Toy Kitchen

Noelle got this as a gift from her grandma when she was around 1. She was able to play with it right away but is getting much better at using the different features.

Baby Doll

Noelle absolutely loves her baby dolls. She has at least 8 and loves them all so much. This one is one of her favorites and I think it's partially because it's small enough that she can carry her all around. I love this brand because they multiple different ethnicities and races. It's important that not all of your child's dolls look exactly like them.

Water Table

This water table can literally keep Noelle busy for HOURS on a hot day. She loves pouring water from one bucket to another, and then onto the ground or herself. It's really neat and a good sprinkler alternative for a young toddler because they will be covered in water within minutes! Haha

Melissa and Doug Cleaning Set

Noelle loves to pretend she's cleaning the house! I like it too becuase I think it's helping her develop some real life skills. She likes to help with the real broom too, but these are much easier to manage and a lot more fun for her!

Velcro Food

I originally ordered these because I thought they'd be a great prop for our food stand tiktok's but Noelle has learned so much from this toy!! She has learned how to use velcro, how to match two things together, and even how to cut! It comes with a very dull fake knife that you use to cut the pieces of food and she mastered it within 2 days! I think it's really teaching her how to cut food safely, maybe she will be able to help prep dinner soon!
