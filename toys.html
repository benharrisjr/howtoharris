While the material doesn't feel that great, I'm hoping once I put them on the pillows they'll feel a little better. They art work looks really nice but I'll give a better review once I put them out!

Cheap Gnomes

This is going to be the weirdest recommendation ever, BUT.. these are very low quality. They are really cheap but they are so cute and I love them!! I woudn't pay more than a few bucks for them but at the price I paid I am really happy! I got one in each color. If the price rises above a few dollars though I'd skip them.

Christopher Radko Ornament

When I was a kid my "fancy" aunt as I called her used to buy me a Christopher Radko hand made Christmas ornament each year. I always felt proud that she trusted me with such a delicate, beautiful ornament. I cherish them and hang them on my tree every year.

Nutcrackers

I don't have these particular nutcrackers, but I am eyeing them. They have good reviews and they're a really good way to decorate a shelf or table that's missing something festive.
